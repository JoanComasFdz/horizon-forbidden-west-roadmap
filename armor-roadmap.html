Phase 1

The Daunt

Lv 3–7 · Uncommon Tier
Your starting armor is better than you think. The Nora Anointed has relevant Acid resistance for Bristlebacks and healing bonuses — don't replace it prematurely. Chainscrape sells two alternatives, but only the Oseram Explorer is worth considering if you're committed to melee. Save your shards.

Phase 2

Into the Forbidden West (Post-Igniter)

Lv 7–17 · Rare Tier
Two essential purchases reshape your loadout. The Nora Sentinel at Barren Light is the first mandatory armor upgrade — broad resistances and Concentration skills. The Carja Shadow at Plainsong is widely considered the best mid-game armor in the entire game. Grab both, plus the free Utaru Whisperer during "The Dying Lands."

Phase 4

The Legendary Grind (Post-Vine Cutter)

Phase 5

Sunwing & Endgame

Lv 30–50 · Final Legendaries + Burning Shores DLC
Sunwing mounts make farming Apex machine parts dramatically easier. Your core trio should be established: Nora Thunder Warrior (ranged), Oseram Artificer (melee/tank), Utaru Winterweave (stealth). The Regalla choice and Burning Shores DLC add the final pieces.

Weave System & Optimization

How outfit modifications work
Weaves are fully swappable and never consumed — remove one, slot another, no penalty. They come in two types: resistance weaves (flat elemental/physical defense, up to 33% for primary elements) and skill weaves (+1 or +2 to specific abilities). All outfits max at 2 weave slots. Unique +2 skill weaves are unlocked by upgrading Legendary armors to level 3.
Best Weaves (Priority Order)
①
+2 Valor Surge Master
Doubles valor gain, enabling near-permanent Valor Surge uptime. Unlocked from upgrading specific Legendary outfits. The single most impactful weave in the game.
S-TIER
②
+2 Concentration+
Essential for ranged builds. Extends slow-motion aiming dramatically. Pairs with the Nora Thunder Warrior's existing Concentration skills to hit the cap without spending skill points.
S-TIER
③
Elemental Resistance Weaves
Patch your armor's specific weakness. Example: slot Plasma Defence into the Nora Thunder Warrior to cover its only meaningful gap. Stack the resistance your current encounter demands.
A-TIER
④
+2 Melee Damage / +2 Critical Strike+
Bonus weaves from Oseram Artificer level 3 upgrade. Massive melee DPS boost. Move them to any outfit for a melee-focused build.
A-TIER
Key rule: Skills cap at level 4 — the skill tree provides up to level 2, and gear plus weaves provide the remaining +2. A Legendary armor with +2 to a skill can max it out without you spending a single skill point. Plan your skill tree around your armor's built-in bonuses.

Ultra Hard & Community Tier List

What experienced players actually do
On Ultra Hard, elemental matching becomes critical — wearing −24 Fire resistance against a Scorcher is a death sentence. Maintain a 3–4 outfit rotation and swap by encounter.

Is Legendary worth the grind? On Story through Hard, fully upgraded Very Rare outfits clear all content. On Very Hard and Ultra Hard, Legendary skill synergies — particularly the +2 bonus weaves unlocked at level 3 — create multiplicative effects that purple armors can't replicate. The grind is worth it on higher difficulties.
No armors are missable (except Tenakth High Marshal / Regalla's Wrath — mutually exclusive by choice). Every other armor remains permanently purchasable or earnable. The game resets to just before the final mission after credits.
