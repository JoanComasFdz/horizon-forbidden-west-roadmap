Phase 1 — The Daunt

Lv 3–7 · Uncommon Tier
Your starting armor is better than you think. The Nora Anointed has relevant Acid resistance for Bristlebacks and healing bonuses — don't replace it prematurely. Save your shards for Phase 2.

Weave System & Optimization

Reference
Weaves are fully swappable and never consumed — remove one, slot another, no penalty. Two types: resistance weaves (up to 33% elemental defense) and skill weaves (+1 or +2 to abilities). All outfits max at 2 weave slots. Unique +2 skill weaves unlock from upgrading Legendaries to level 3.
Best Weaves (Priority Order)
①
+2 Valor Surge Master Skill Weave
Doubles valor gain — near-permanent Valor Surge uptime. Most impactful weave in the game.
②
+2 Concentration+ Skill Weave
Essential for ranged builds. Pairs with Thunder Warrior's Concentration skills to hit the cap without spending skill points.
③
Elemental Resistance Weaves Defense Weave
Patch your armor's weakness. Example: Plasma Defence into Thunder Warrior to cover its only meaningful gap. Swap per encounter.
④
+2 Melee Damage / +2 Critical Strike+ Skill Weave
Bonus weaves from Oseram Artificer level 3. Massive melee DPS boost. Transferable to any outfit.
Key rule: Skills cap at level 4 — skill tree provides up to 2, gear + weaves provide the remaining +2. A Legendary armor with +2 to a skill can max it out without spending a single skill point. Plan your skill tree around your armor's built-in bonuses.

Community Armor Tier List

Reference
On Ultra Hard, elemental matching is critical — wearing −24 Fire resist against a Scorcher is a death sentence. Maintain 3–4 outfits and swap by encounter. Rankings from Reddit, Steam, ScreenRant, GameRant, and community consensus.
S
Nora Thunder Warrior S-Tier
Best all-rounder. HFW is a ranged game — nothing else extends Concentration as much. Default for every non-stealth, non-melee encounter.

Oseram Artificer S-Tier
Highest defense + best melee. With Overshield Valor Surge = closest to Shield-Weaver. Mandatory for melee builds.
S
Utaru Winterweave S-Tier
Definitive stealth armor. 85% overhealth on Silent Strike kills. Plasma resistance covers Thunder Warrior's weakness.
S
Quen Marine S-Tier · DLC
Thunder Warrior sidegrade. Trades Heavy Weapon+ for Valor Surge Master. Equal power, different emphasis.
A
Quen Deadeye · Quen Commander A-Tier · DLC
Deadeye for stealth archery. Commander for Resonator Strikes with less defense than Artificer. Both excellent, slightly behind S-tier.
B
Tenakth Tactician · Carja Stalker Elite B-Tier
Niche specialists. Tactician for overrides; Stalker Elite for traps. Useful in their lanes, narrow otherwise.
C
Tenakth Vanquisher · Nora Lookout C-Tier · Avoid
Community "noob traps." Vanquisher's low-HP bonuses are suicidal on Ultra Hard. Lookout only helps when already losing. Skip both.
Is Legendary worth the grind? On Story through Hard, fully upgraded Very Rare outfits clear all content. On Very Hard and Ultra Hard, Legendary skill synergies — particularly +2 bonus weaves at level 3 — create multiplicative effects purple armors can't replicate.
No armors are missable (except Tenakth High Marshal / Regalla's Wrath — mutually exclusive by choice). Every other armor remains permanently purchasable or earnable. The game resets to just before the final mission after credits.
