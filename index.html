Phase 1 — The Daunt (Prologue)

Lv 3–7
Linear opening. Focus on the main story to learn controls and reach the Forbidden West. Pick up early side quests in Chainscrape — they give great XP, skills, and one of the best early weapons.
1
Reach for the Stars Main
Tutorial mission. Explore the launch facility with Varl. Unlocks: Pullcaster
2
The Point of the Lance Interlude
Return to Meridian. Investigate the Spire. Story exposition.
3
To the Brink Main
Enter The Daunt. Unlocks merchants and the first settlement, Chainscrape. Unlocks: Shieldwing (glider)
Side Content — Chainscrape (do before The Embassy)
▸
A Bigger Boom Errand
Two Oseram tinkers on the way out of Chainscrape. Fun dialogue and rewards one of the best early weapons — a Spike Thrower. Reward: Spike Thrower
▸
Deep Trouble Side Quest
Investigate a flooded mine. Good early XP and introduces underwater mechanics.
▸
The Bristlebacks Side Quest
Help a local town with its problems. Reward: Nora Champion outfit
▸
The Twilight Path Side Quest
Talk to Petra in Chainscrape. Great story about investigating a Carja camp. Good XP and skill points.
4
The Embassy Main
Attend the Embassy between the Carja and Tenakth. Crosses into the Forbidden West proper. Unlocks: Access to the Forbidden West

Phase 2 — Into the Forbidden West

Lv 7–17
Push through main quests to unlock the Igniter (Firegleam tool) and reach The Base. Do side content in Barren Light, Plainsong, and No Man's Land along the way. Start Salvage Contracts with Keruf in Barren Light early — there are 17 total and they lead to a legendary armor set.
5
Death's Door Main
Track Sylens' signal. Critical progression mission. Unlocks: Igniter (detonates Firegleam / red crystals)
Side Content — Barren Light & No Man's Land
▸
Shadow from the Past → Shadow in the West Side Quest Chain
Eclipse quest chain from HZD. Great for Zero Dawn fans — wraps up lingering story threads. Start at Barren Light.
▸
Breaking Even Side Quest
Missable! During this quest, do the optional objective to recover Porguf's Lockbox. If you skip it, the errand "Broken Locks" becomes permanently missable (needed for 100%).
▸
Start Salvage Contracts (Keruf, Barren Light) Activity
Begin early — there are 17 contracts total across four NPCs. Completing all of them rewards the Oseram Artificer, the best melee legendary armor. Legendary: Oseram Artificer armor
▸
Cauldron: MU Cauldron
South of Plainsong. First optional cauldron — unlocks overrides for common machines (Scrounger, Burrower, Scrapper, etc.). Do it early for useful overrides.
▸
The Roots That Bind Side Quest
Plainsong area. Lengthy quest with tough fights, platforming, and a stealth section. Good XP.
▸
The Promontory Side Quest
Plainsong. Reveals the Utaru's inner struggles. Rich story, no major gear reward but excellent narrative.
▸
Tallnecks (any you encounter) Activity
Always override Tallnecks when you find them — they reveal large portions of the map for free, showing quest locations and points of interest.
6
The Dying Lands Main
Venture into Cauldron TAU (story-required). Capture MINERVA sub-function. Unlocks the Bristleback mount. Unlocks: Cauldron TAU overrides + Bristleback mount
7
The Eye of the Earth Interlude
Establish The Base — your hub for the rest of the game. Fabrication Terminal for crafting overrides. Unlocks: The Base, Fabrication Terminal, Gauntlet Runs, 3-way branch

Phase 3 — The Three Branches (Recommended Order)

Lv 17–24
After The Eye of the Earth, three quests unlock simultaneously. You can do them in any order, but each one unlocks a critical exploration tool. The recommended order prioritizes tools that open the most content:

1st: The Sea of Sands (Poseidon) → Diving Mask for unlimited underwater exploration
2nd: Seeds of the Past (Demeter) → Vine Cutter to clear Metal Flower blockages
3rd: The Broken Sky → The Kulrut (Aether) → Tear Arrows + Arena access

⚠ Important: Completing any ONE of these three triggers Cradle of Echoes, which locks you in until completed. So do side content between branches if you want.
① Poseidon Branch — Do First
8
The Sea of Sands Main
Travel to the ruins of Las Vegas. Retrieve POSEIDON. Unlocks: Diving Mask (unlimited underwater breathing) This opens up underwater caves, Relic Ruins with submerged sections, and several side quests that were impossible before.
⟳
Cradle of Echoes Main — Auto-triggered
Automatically starts after completing any branch. You're locked in until it's done. Short mission chasing HADES. Unlocks: Firegleam tool becomes available in more areas
Side Content Break (with Diving Mask)
▸
Drowned Hopes Side Quest
Requires Diving Mask. Rewards a blue-tier Tripcaster. Now accessible with your new underwater ability.
▸
Signal Spike Side Quest
Requires Diving Mask. Help an Oseram tinker decipher a signal. Rewards a Spike Thrower.
▸
Nights of Lights Errand
In The Stillsands (near Las Vegas). Uncovers an ornament from Relic Ruins. Required for the legendary Ancestor's Return shredder gauntlet alongside the 9 Relic Ruins.
▸
Relic Ruins (any accessible ones) Activity
Start collecting Ornaments. Some need the Vine Cutter (wait until you have it). Completing all 9 + Nights of Lights → Legendary: Ancestor's Return (Shredder Gauntlet)
② Demeter Branch — Do Second
9
Seeds of the Past Main
Travel to the far western coast, The Greenhouse. Retrieve DEMETER. Unlocks: Vine Cutter (clears Metal Flower blockages) Opens blocked paths across the entire map, required for some Relic Ruins and side areas.
⟳
Cradle of Echoes Auto-triggered again
If not yet done, triggers here. Otherwise this step is skipped.
Side Content Break (with Vine Cutter + Diving Mask)
▸
The Burning Blooms Side Quest
Track down Talanah from HZD. First part of a chain. Start this before it chains into "Need to Know" (available after Cradle of Echoes).
▸
Remaining Relic Ruins Activity
With both Diving Mask and Vine Cutter, you can now complete all 9 Relic Ruins. Return Ornaments to Stemmur in Hidden Ember for the legendary reward.
▸
Cauldron: CHI Cauldron
Southeast of Thornmarsh. Good overrides. Accessible now with your full toolkit.
③ Aether Branch — Do Third
10
The Broken Sky Main
Travel to Memorial Grove to meet Chief Hekarro. Sets up the next mission. Unlocks: Tear Arrows (strip machine components)
11
The Kulrut Main
Major battle event. Retrieve AETHER. Unlocks: Arena access (via "Opening the Arena" side quest) The Arena is where you earn legendary weapons and armor for medals.
⟳
Cradle of Echoes Auto-triggered if not done
Final chance — if somehow still not triggered, it happens now.

Phase 4 — Mid-Game Exploration Window

Lv 20–30
With all three tools unlocked (Diving Mask, Vine Cutter, Igniter) and Arena access, this is the best time to do side content. You can access nearly every area and earn powerful gear before the final stretch.
Priority Side Quests
▸
Thirst for the Hunt → The Wound in the Sand → The Gate of the Vanquished → The Deluge Side Quest Chain
Arrowhand settlement chain. Includes a meaningful player choice (Drakka vs Yarra). Rewards the Tenakth Vindicator — one of the best mid-game armors (stealth-friendly perks and great stats). Reward: Tenakth Vindicator outfit
▸
Need to Know Side Quest
Continuation of "The Burning Blooms" (Talanah chain). Available after Cradle of Echoes. Great story featuring a Tideripper fight.
▸
Blood for Blood Side Quest
Hefty battle and heartwarming story. Reward: Perimeter Tripcaster
▸
A Tribe Apart Side Quest
Help young Tenakth warriors. Required for "Broken Locks" errand to appear.
▸
Broken Locks Errand
Missable! Only available if you recovered Porguf's Lockbox during Breaking Even. Check Camp Nowhere for the quest marker.
▸
Opening the Arena Side Quest
Fetch quest at The Maw of the Arena (after The Kulrut). Unlocks Arena challenges where you earn medals for legendary gear. Unlocks: Arena Challenges
Activities & Legendary Weapon Paths
▸
Rebel Camps (all 5 → 6th "First Forge") Activity
Clear all 5 rebel camps, then talk to Erend to unlock the 6th secret camp "First Forge". Boss Asera drops a legendary bow. Legendary: The Sun Scourge (Hunter Bow)
▸
Cauldron: IOTA Cauldron
North of Scalding Spear. One of the best — contains a hidden Tallneck inside, revealing a huge map section. Wide variety of overrides. Unlocks: Tallneck: Salt Bite + many overrides
▸
Cauldron: KAPPA Cauldron
Requires Diving Mask (underwater entrance). Hardest cauldron — unlocks overrides for the most powerful machines. West of The Bulwark along the coast.
▸
Black Boxes (all 12) Collectible
Find all 12, trade them with Untalla at Memorial Grove. Legendary: Wings of the Ten (Blastsling)
▸
Hunting Grounds (all 4, Full Stripes) Activity
Complete all 12 trials with Full Stripes rating. Legendary: Tinker's Pride (Tripcaster)
▸
Gauntlet Runs (all 4) Activity
Machine races. First one near Scalding Spear. Complete all four. Legendary: Carja's Bane (Warrior Bow)
▸
Arena — Grind for Medals Activity
80 medals → Death-Seeker's Shadow (Hunter Bow) + Forgefall (Sharpshot Bow). 54 medals each → legendary armor sets (Nora Thunder Warrior, Carja Stalker Elite, Tenakth Vanquisher). Can be done on any difficulty.
▸
Melee Pits (all 4 + final Enduring) Activity
Complete the 4 Melee Pits, then challenge The Enduring for the trophy. Spread across different regions — do as you encounter them.
▸
First to Fly Errand
Reassemble the armor of the first Tenakth to take flight. Reward: Tenakth Sky Climber outfit (concentration + healing)

Phase 5 — Late Story (Faro's Tomb → GEMINI)

Lv 26–31
Return to the main story. The Gemini mission unlocks the Sunwing override (flying) — the game's biggest mobility upgrade. This opens several late-game side quests. Scan Specters! — they only appear in a few main quests. Scan them with your Focus for the trophy.
12
Faro's Tomb Main
Major lore mission. Explore Ted Faro's bunker. Level 26 recommended.
13
GEMINI Main
Cauldron GEMINI — trap HEPHAESTUS. Story-required cauldron. Unlocks: Sunwing Override = FLYING! This is a massive exploration upgrade. Opens up late-game side quests and lets you reach areas you couldn't before.
14
All That Remains Interlude
Learn the truth about the Zeniths and the Odyssey. Story exposition at The Base.
▼

Phase 6 — Final Exploration Window (Before Endgame)

Lv 30–35+
With flying unlocked, this is your last best window to clean up everything before the finale. Companion quests at The Base are especially recommended — they have emotional payoffs and trophies. Do the rebel camps before Wings of the Ten for narrative coherence (you're fighting Regalla's rebellion, so clearing her camps first makes sense).
Companion Quests (from The Base)
★
The Second Verse (Zo) Companion Quest
Widely considered the most emotionally beautiful quest in the game. Heal the Land Gods. Changes the environment. Highly recommended before Singularity. Reward: Utaru Warden outfit + trophy
★
What Was Lost (Kotallo) Companion Quest
Help Kotallo test his new mechanical arm. Great emotional payoff. Do before Singularity for narrative coherence. Reward: Trophy
★
Forbidden Legacy (Alva) Companion Quest
Help Alva recover data. Rewards a strong survivability outfit. Reward: Carja Behemoth Trapper outfit + trophy
Late-Game Side Content (Requires Flying)
▸
The Way Home Side Quest
Requires Sunwing (flying). Legacy's Landfall. Talk to the women on the boat. Rewards one of the best weapons in the entire game. Legendary: The Skykiller (Spike Thrower)
▸
The Valley of the Fallen Side Quest
Late-game quest tied closely to the main story. Features Thunderjaw, Tremortusk, and Specter fights. Best experienced before Singularity — machine encounters change if done after.
▸
A Soldier's March Side Quest
Help a soldier on a march. Good XP and story.
▸
A Bigger Boom — Part 2 (revisit the sisters) Errand
Return to the Oseram sisters from early game. They gift you another excellent weapon. Reward: Boomer's Shredder Gauntlet
▸
Finish any remaining activities Cleanup
Remaining Rebel Outposts, Tallnecks, Vista Points, Survey Drones. Check your Notebook for anything incomplete.

Phase 7 — Endgame

Lv 32–35
Two final missions. Wings of the Ten includes a major boss fight and the Regalla choice. Singularity is the point of no return (though you can continue playing after credits). Scan all Specters with your Focus during these missions!
15
The Wings of the Ten Main
War against Regalla at Memorial Grove. Major boss fight. Afterward you must make the Regalla choice:
Kill Regalla → Get Regalla's Wrath (Very Rare Sharpshot Bow, strong but outclassed by Arena's Forgefall later)
Spare Regalla → Get Tenakth High Marshal outfit + she fights alongside you in the finale and gets a warrior's death (better narrative payoff). She dies either way — sparing gives a more satisfying arc.
16
Singularity Main — Final Mission
Storm the Zenith base. Point of no return when you "Call Your Companions at the Campfire." After credits, the world remains open — you can continue all side content. No quests are permanently locked out.

Post-credits
After Singularity, the world stays open. All side quests remain available (some dialogue may differ slightly). The Burning Shores DLC only starts after completing Singularity. Note: Burning Shores was originally PS5-exclusive but is included in the PC Complete Edition.

Any remaining side content Cleanup
